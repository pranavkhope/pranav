Early Life

Maharana Pratap was born to Maharana Udai Singh II, the ruler of Mewar, and his queen, Maharani Jaiwanta Bai. He was trained in martial arts and warfare from a young age, preparing him for the challenges he would face as a ruler.

Legacy

Maharana Pratap's legacy is one of courage and resilience. He is remembered for his unwavering commitment to his kingdom and his people. His famous battle against Akbar's forces at Haldighati in 1576 is a testament to his bravery and strategic acumen.
